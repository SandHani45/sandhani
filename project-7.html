TruckerUnion

Client : TruckerUnion
Completion : July 2018 - Present
Skils : HTML5,CSS3,BOOTSTRAP,JAVASCRIPT,React/Redux,Node Js,Express js

With Trucker Union users can now get their luggage picked up by logging in to the app booking a truck and setting the drop off location. One among the numerous trucks will go through the entire task of dropping off your luggage as assigned by you through the Trucker Union app.
